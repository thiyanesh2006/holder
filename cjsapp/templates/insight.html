Understanding Customer Journey Mapping

Customer journey mapping is a powerful tool that helps businesses understand the experiences of their customers. By visualizing the steps a customer takes from initial contact to final purchase, companies can identify pain points and opportunities for improvement.

Key Stages of Customer Journey

Awareness

The customer becomes aware of a need or problem and starts looking for solutions.

Consideration

The customer evaluates different options and compares products or services.

Decision

The customer makes a purchase decision and completes the transaction.

Customer Journey Mapping Tools

Tool 1: Journey Mapper

A comprehensive tool that allows businesses to create detailed customer journey maps.

Tool 2: Experience Tracker

Helps businesses track and analyze customer interactions across touchpoints.
